Ideas and Tea

My name's Boris, I'm originally from Ukraine, live between California & Boston, and trying to make the most of life.

For whatever reason, I have an inexplicable desire to share - everything that I know, have found, done, seen, experienced, discovered.. everything! My ultimate goal is to create a platform/method for sharing everything I possibly can - and this site is a first stab at that (a 'rough draft' of sorts). For now, this site is a little slice of my life: the things I've done, places I've been, and what I've learned in the process.

Why 'IdeasandTea'?

Probably the only two things that have been constant in the past four years of my life are my love for ideas, and my love for tea. Plus, why not? ;)
